How It Works

Three steps to mastery.

Simple to start. Profound over time.

Step 01

Take a 5-minute diagnostic

Answer a short adaptive quiz. The AI pinpoints exactly where you stand in each 100-level course — no guessing, no wasted time on things you already understand.

Step 02

Get your Skill Map

The engine builds a visual map of your strengths and gaps. It orders your curriculum by what you need most — not by chapter number. Your path is unique to you.

Watch mastery grow

As you practice, your rings fill. The AI Advisor adjusts difficulty, predicts your mastery dates, and tells you exactly when you're ready for the next level.
